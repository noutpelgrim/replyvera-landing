Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

At ReplyVera, we value your privacy and the trust you place in us to manage your online reputation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better service to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and basic profile information via Google OAuth.
  • Google Business Profile Data: To operate our core service, we request access to your Google Business Profile. This includes review content, reviewer names, and location data.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our dashboard to improve our user interface and AI performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To draft and post replies to your Google reviews using AI.
  • To provide analytics on your review performance.
  • To communicate with you about your account and service updates.
  • To improve our AI algorithms for more accurate and helpful responses.

3. Limited Use for Google User Data

ReplyVera's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not use your Google data for serving advertisements, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. If you disconnect your Google Account or delete your ReplyVera account, we will remove your tokens and cease data collection immediately.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption at rest and in transit, to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@replyvera.com.
