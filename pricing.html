FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReplyVera pricing, plans, and capabilities.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. ReplyVera is billed monthly, and you can cancel before your next billing date.

Is there a setup fee?

No. There is no setup fee for standard plans.

Does each location need its own plan?

Starter and Autopilot include one location. Multi-location plans include several locations and allow additional locations to be added.

Are review responses unlimited?

The Starter plan includes a monthly response allowance (30 replies). Autopilot, Multi-Location, and Agency plans include unlimited responses subject to reasonable-use limits.

Which review platforms are included?

ReplyVera currently supports Google Reviews. Facebook, Trustpilot, Booking.com, and Tripadvisor are planned for future releases.

Can I approve responses before they are published?

Yes. Manual approval is included, and Autopilot users can decide which types of reviews may be published automatically.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Customers can upgrade or downgrade their plan as their review volume or number of locations changes.
