FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReplyVera for Restaurants.

Can ReplyVera manage multiple restaurant locations?

Yes. Our multi-location controls allow you to manage separate Google Business Profiles from one central dashboard, with unique configurations for each branch.

Can food-safety reviews be blocked from auto-publishing?

Absolutely. ReplyVera identifies words related to allergies, illness, or cleanliness, blocking auto-replies immediately and routing them to a manager approval inbox.

Can ReplyVera recognize employees?

Yes. It scans reviews for names and cross-references them with your staff roster, logging top mentions to help reward team performance.

Can it respond in Spanish?

Yes. ReplyVera automatically detects the language of the review and drafts responses in both natural English and Spanish.

Does ReplyVera support Yelp or OpenTable?

Currently supported: Google Reviews. Support for Facebook, Trustpilot, Booking.com, and Tripadvisor are planned for future releases. Yelp and OpenTable are not supported.
