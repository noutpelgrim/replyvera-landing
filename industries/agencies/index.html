FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReplyVera for Marketing Agencies.

Can every client have a different brand voice?

Yes. Every business location or profile has its own settings. You can configure a unique tone, automatic publish criteria, and approval workflows.

Can clients approve responses without accessing the full dashboard?

Yes. ReplyVera generates secure client approval links. Clients can approve or edit drafts from their phone without full system access.

Can team members manage assigned clients?

Yes. Our agency portal lets you assign specific staff members or account managers to specific client profiles.

Can the agency resell ReplyVera?

Absolutely. You can package ReplyVera's review automation as a premium add-on to your local SEO or reputation services.

Which platforms are supported?

We currently support Google Reviews. Integrations for Facebook, Trustpilot, Booking.com, and Tripadvisor are planned for future agency releases.
