Professional Online Reputation Management Without Patient Privacy Violations

Managing reviews shouldn't eat into your day. Here are the core issues we solve.

Staff may accidentally confirm private information

Verifying a reviewer's active patient status or referencing their dental details online can lead to serious privacy concerns.

Clinical complaints require careful wording

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReplyVera for Dentists & Clinics.

Does ReplyVera reveal patient information?

No. ReplyVera is programmed to draft privacy-conscious responses that never confirm active patient status or discuss clinical treatments.

Can clinical complaints be excluded from auto-publishing?

Yes. Any review mentioning pain, procedures, side effects, or clinical outcomes is flagged and held for manual review by your dental team.

Can multiple practice locations be managed?

Yes. You can link all clinic Business Profiles to one central account and manage permission levels for office staff.

Can staff members approve replies?

Yes. Roster dentists, coordinators, or clinic leads can log in and approve drafts with one click.

Which review platforms are supported?

Currently supported: Google Reviews. Facebook, Trustpilot, Booking.com, and Tripadvisor are planned for future releases.
