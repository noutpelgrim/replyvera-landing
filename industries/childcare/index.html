FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReplyVera for Childcare & Preschool.

Does ReplyVera reveal child information?

No. ReplyVera is programmed to write warm but generic replies that never confirm child identity, enrollment status, or program details, keeping privacy safe.

Can supervision or allergy complaints be blocked from auto-publishing?

Yes. Any review containing words related to injuries, supervision, medication, or food allergies is immediately locked in drafts for director review.

Can multiple daycare locations be managed?

Yes. Our platform connects multiple daycare Business Profiles, allowing group operators or franchise directors to manage review feeds from one dashboard.

Does it support Spanish reviews?

Yes. ReplyVera handles responses in both English and Spanish, replying to parents in their written language.

Which review platforms are supported?

ReplyVera currently supports Google Reviews, which is key for local preschool search rankings. Facebook, Trustpilot, Booking.com, and Tripadvisor are planned for future releases.
