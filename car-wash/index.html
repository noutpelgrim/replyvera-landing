FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about integrations, autopilots and cancellations.

How does the Google integration work?

We use Google's official Secure OAuth API to connect directly to your Business Profile. This does not store your passwords, and you can revoke permissions in your Google account settings at any time.

Can I approve replies before they go live?

Yes. By default, ReplyVera operates in a safe draft approval mode. The AI generates responses and queues them in your inbox. You can review, make quick edits, and approve them with one click, or toggle full autopilot for positive reviews.

What reviews can be published automatically?

You have full control. Most businesses choose to auto-post replies for 5-star ratings to save maximum time, while routing lower ratings (1-3 stars) to a manual approval inbox for customized handling.

Which review platforms does ReplyVera support?

ReplyVera currently supports Google Reviews through Google Business Profile. Facebook, Trustpilot, Booking.com, and Tripadvisor are planned for future releases but are not currently available.

Can I join a waitlist for future platforms?

Yes. Customers can register their interest in future platform integrations, and ReplyVera can notify them when new integrations become available.

How can I get help if I have questions?

We are here for you! You can reach us anytime at support@replyvera.com. As a lean team, we typically respond within 1 business day.
